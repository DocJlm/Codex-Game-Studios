# Store Page Draft

## Short Description

Deliver moon-bound parcels through a folklore town where light is the enemy. Plan the route, dodge patrol lamps, spend strange tools, and finish each delivery before dawn.

## Long Description

Moonlight Dispatch is a compact 2D route-planning and light-stealth game about moving packages that must never be seen by the wrong light. Each night is a short delivery puzzle across a foggy town: study the map, choose a route, avoid patrol lamps, unlock shortcuts, and decide when to spend your limited tools.

Between jobs, the town's courier network reveals why these parcels matter and why everyone is afraid of morning.

## Feature Bullets

- 12 authored deliveries across 4 compact districts.
- Short 8-12 minute nights with quick retries.
- Route-first stealth: win by reading the city, not fighting it.
- Four limited-use courier tools that open risky shortcuts.
- Cozy-dark story told through dispatch notes, NPCs, and delivery outcomes.
- Daily Challenge mode for replayable route puzzles.
