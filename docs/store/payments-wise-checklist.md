# Payments And Wise Checklist

This document tracks public setup steps only. Do not commit tax identifiers, bank account numbers, Wise account details, legal IDs, or private account screenshots.

## Steam

- Create or use Steamworks partner account.
- Complete legal name, tax, and payment setup in Steamworks.
- Confirm whether Wise-provided account details are accepted for the target currency and country.
- Expected payment rails from public docs: ACH or USD SWIFT depending on account details and region.
- Record only non-sensitive status in project notes.

## itch.io

- Choose payment mode:
  - Direct to you.
  - Collected by itch.io.
- Verify payout method availability for the account region.
- Use private page first for install and payment-flow testing.

## Wise

- Use Wise account details only as a candidate receiving account.
- Verify the exact currency account details required by each platform.
- Do not commit account numbers, routing numbers, IBANs, tax forms, or legal identity docs.

## Launch Readiness

- Store payment setup complete.
- Tax forms accepted.
- Test purchase path understood for itch.io.
- Steam review timing accounted for.
