# Moonlight Dispatch Art Bible

## Visual Identity

Moonlight Dispatch should feel like a hand-painted night map seen through fogged glass: readable silhouettes, warm window light, blue moon haze, and courier tools that glow just enough to guide the player.

## Style Keywords

- Cozy dark
- Moonlit folklore
- Compact town mystery
- Painterly 2D with crisp gameplay silhouettes
- Soft fog, hard light hazards

## Palette

| Role | Color Direction |
| --- | --- |
| Moonlight | pale blue, desaturated cyan |
| Safe alleys | muted violet, charcoal, soft gray |
| Danger light | warm amber, dusty gold |
| Courier accent | red scarf or satchel mark |
| UI highlight | moon-white with subtle blue edge |

## Shape Language

- Courier: small triangular silhouette, satchel, scarf, readable at zoomed-out scale.
- Town: crooked roofs, narrow alleys, hanging signs, round lamps.
- Hazards: cones and arcs of warm light with crisp boundaries.
- Tools: simple iconic silhouettes that can become UI buttons.

## Asset Strategy

- Use generated concept art for direction, not final unreviewed production assets.
- Prefer modular 2D tiles and props over bespoke full-screen illustrations.
- Store art can be more painterly than in-game art but must show the real gameplay promise.

## First Visual Targets

- Key art: courier crossing moonlit street with package and patrol light.
- Steam capsule concept: title-safe composition with strong silhouette.
- Character sheet: courier, three NPC archetypes, and tool silhouettes.
