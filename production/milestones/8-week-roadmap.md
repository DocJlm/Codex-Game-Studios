# 8 Week Roadmap

## Week 1 - Project Foundation And Visual Direction

- Complete GDD, systems index, technical preferences, art bible, architecture, epics, stories, store draft, and concept art.
- Gate: concept has a clear paid hook and feasible scope.

## Week 2 - Core Prototype

- Implement movement, job start, route target, countdown, delivery completion, and first graybox map.
- Smoke: player can complete one delivery.

## Week 3 - Vertical Slice

- Produce one 3-5 minute near-production delivery.
- Add light patrol, one tool, HUD, audio placeholders, and debrief.
- Verdict: PROCEED, PIVOT, or KILL.

## Week 4 - Content Pipeline

- Expand to six deliveries, two districts, and two NPCs.
- Lock delivery data format.
- Start Steam page media script.

## Week 5 - Commercial Demo

- Create 15-20 minute Steam Next Fest style demo.
- Test itch.io private page package.
- Add tutorial, settings, and feedback fixes.

## Week 6 - Full Content

- Complete twelve deliveries, four districts, story ending, daily challenge, English copy, and Simplified Chinese localization draft.

## Week 7 - Polish And Store Prep

- Polish menu, pause, HUD, debrief, failure feedback, achievements draft, screenshots, trailer script, Steam capsule, and store descriptions.

## Week 8 - Release Candidate

- Produce Windows and macOS builds.
- Run QA plan, smoke check, release checklist, and gate check.
- Publish itch.io paid page and submit or schedule Steam release if approved.
