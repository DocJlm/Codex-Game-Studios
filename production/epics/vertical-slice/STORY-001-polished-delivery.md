# Story: Polished Delivery Slice

Status: Ready
Owner role: level-designer

## Context

Create one representative delivery route that can be finished in 3-5 minutes.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Route includes at least two meaningful path choices.
- Route includes one patrol light and one route blockage.
- Delivery can be completed from briefing to debrief.
- Route supports at least one optional time-saving shortcut.

## Test Plan

- Manual playthrough: complete the delivery without guidance.
- Record completion time and moments of confusion.
