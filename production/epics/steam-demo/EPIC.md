# Epic: Steam Demo

Goal: Build a 15-20 minute public demo suitable for Steam Next Fest style discovery and itch.io private testing.

## Success Criteria

- Demo includes tutorial, three deliveries, one NPC thread, settings, and end card.
- Demo can be packaged for Windows and macOS.
- Demo has store-ready screenshots and trailer capture plan.
